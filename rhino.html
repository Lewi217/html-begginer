THE RHINO

The Rhino

  • Rhinos have massive bodies and a large head with 1-2 horns.

  • The horns are dermal in origin; they are very solid and are composed of compressed,fibrous keratin.

  • Rhinos have a broad chest and short ,stumpy legs.

  • The radius/ulna and tibia/fibula are only slightly moveable, but they are well developed and separate.

  • Both hind and forefeet are mesaxonic with 3 digits each; each digit with a small hoof
