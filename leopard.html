THE LEOPARD

The Leopard

  • Leopard are canivores

  • They are nocturnal animals and do most of their hunting at night.

  • Their large eyes and dilated pupils allow them to see well in dark conditions.

  • Leopards are incredibly athletic and known for their climbing ability.

  • They often carry food into trees to avoid losing it to scavengers like lions and hyenas
