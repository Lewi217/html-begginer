The Buffalo

    width="400" height="225">

  • Have horns that are triangular-shaped in cross-section.

  • Stout legs to support its ox-like body.

  • Capable of running at speeds up to 48-56km/hour,enough to outrun lions.

  • No scent glands.

  • Loss of of body water through evaporation can be reduced by allowing the body temperature to gradually rise through the day
